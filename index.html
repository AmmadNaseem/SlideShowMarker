FAQ

Can I add my own music?

Yes, upload your own songs or audio tracks from the device storage for personal use.

Can I make a slideshow offline?

Yes, the Windows app works without an active connection. The online version requires internet access.

What image formats can I use?

The tool supports most popular formats like JPG, PNG, BMP, TIFF, GIF, and more, so you can import photos from virtually any device or camera.

Is there a limit on the number of photos?

No, you can add as many images as you like. The only limit is your device's storage capacity.

How can I preview the result before exporting it?

Yes, you can watch your picture slideshow at any point during editing so you can make real-time adjustments before saving the final version.
